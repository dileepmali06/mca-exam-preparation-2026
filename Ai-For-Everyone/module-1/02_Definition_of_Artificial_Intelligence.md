# 1. What is Artificial Intelligence?

Artificial Intelligence (AI) is a branch of Computer Science that
focuses on developing intelligent systems capable of performing tasks
that normally require human intelligence.

These tasks include:

-   Learning
-   Reasoning
-   Problem Solving
-   Decision Making
-   Language Understanding
-   Perception

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 2. Meaning of Artificial Intelligence

## Artificial

Artificial means **human-made** or **man-made**, not naturally
occurring.

Examples:

-   Artificial Flowers
-   Artificial Heart
-   Artificial Leather

## Intelligence

Intelligence is the ability to:

-   Learn
-   Think
-   Understand
-   Reason
-   Solve Problems
-   Make Decisions
-   Improve from Experience

## Artificial Intelligence

Artificial Intelligence means **human-made intelligence**, where
machines are designed to perform intelligent tasks similar to humans.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 3. Standard Definition

> Artificial Intelligence is the capability of a computer or machine to
> perform tasks that normally require human intelligence such as
> learning, reasoning, problem solving, perception, language
> understanding, and decision making.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 4. Core Capabilities of AI

## Learning

AI learns from data and improves its performance.

Example: Netflix recommendations.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Reasoning

AI applies logic to reach conclusions.

Example: Medical diagnosis systems.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Problem Solving

AI finds the best solution among multiple possibilities.

Example: Google Maps selecting the fastest route.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Perception

AI understands information from the environment.

Example: Self-driving cars detecting roads and traffic signs.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Language Understanding

AI understands and generates human language.

Example:

-   ChatGPT
-   Google Translate
-   Alexa

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Decision Making

AI makes intelligent decisions based on available data.

Example: Fraud Detection Systems.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 5. Objectives of Artificial Intelligence

The main objectives of AI are:

-   Simulate human intelligence
-   Automate decision making
-   Improve efficiency
-   Reduce human effort
-   Solve complex problems
-   Learn continuously from data

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 6. Definitions by Different Organizations

## Gartner

AI uses advanced analysis and logic-based techniques, including Machine
Learning, to interpret events, automate decisions, and take actions.

## Stanford University

AI is the science and engineering of making intelligent machines.

## Encyclopaedia Britannica

AI is the ability of computers or robots to perform tasks associated
with intelligent beings.
