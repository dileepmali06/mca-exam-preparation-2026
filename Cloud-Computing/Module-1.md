# Cloud Computing - Module 1

## Disaster Recovery in Cloud Computing

### Definition

Disaster Recovery (DR) is a strategy used to restore applications, data, and IT infrastructure after a failure, cyberattack, or natural disaster.

### Objectives

- Minimize downtime
- Protect business data
- Ensure business continuity
- Reduce financial losses

### Benefits

- Faster recovery
- Lower infrastructure cost
- Automatic backups
- High availability

### Types

1. Backup and Restore
2. Pilot Light
3. Warm Standby
4. Multi-Site Active/Active

### Key Terms

- RPO (Recovery Point Objective)
- RTO (Recovery Time Objective)

### Exam Point

Cloud Disaster Recovery provides faster recovery and lower cost compared to traditional disaster recovery methods.
